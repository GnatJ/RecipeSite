Recipe of the Month

Carrot Ginger Soup

Ingredients

  • 1kg carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons crushed ginger
  • 2 teaspoons crushed garlic
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable stock powder

Method

  1. Peel and chop onion
  2. Fry onion in a olive oil in a heavy bottomed pot
  3. Peel and dice carrots into small chunks
  4. Add carrots, ginger and garlic to the pot
  5. Mix the water and the stock powder and add to the pot
  6. Bring to the boil and reduce heat to a simmer for an hour
  7. Remove from heat and allow to cool
  8. Use an immersion blender to blend the soup
  9. Enjoy with fresh bread
